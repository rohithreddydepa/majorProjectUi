Introduction

Stack Overflow is one of the most popular online question-answer forums in the current generation and is growing to be the greatest question-answer gathering for programmers and software developers. Owing to the large amount of information in this forum, to improve the ease of user search, efficient tagging is very necessary. Tagging is the process of identifying the label or class to which the piece of information belongs to. Tagging helps in easy retrieval and organization of data. We present a project that performs tagging for various category of questions using Machine Learning and Natural Language Processing.
